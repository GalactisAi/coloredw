Our Story

Born in Chennai, Built for Indian Homes

COLORED started with a simple belief: every Indian home deserves design that feels personal, aspirational, and achievable. What began as a passion for residential interiors in Chennai has grown into a studio that serves families across India's leading metros.

Our mission is to make high-quality interior design accessible — without the overwhelm. We believe in listening first, then designing. Your lifestyle, your rituals, your dreams for the space — they shape every decision we make.

From compact apartments to sprawling villas, we bring the same attention to detail and warmth to every project. Because at COLORED, we don't just design rooms — we craft the backdrop for your life.

What We Stand For

Our Values

The principles that guide every project we take on — and every relationship we build.

Human-Centric Design

We design around how you live — your routines, your family, your rituals. Every layout and material choice is rooted in your reality.

Detail-Obsessed Execution

From joinery to finish, we sweat the small stuff. Quality that lasts — and looks effortless — is non-negotiable.

Transparent Collaboration

You're part of the process every step of the way. Clear timelines, honest budgets, and no surprises — ever.

Sustainable Choices

We favour materials and practices that tread lightly on the planet — without compromising on aesthetics or durability.

Lasting Relationships

We design for the long term — and so do our relationships. Many clients return for their next home or refer friends.
