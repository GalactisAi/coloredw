Modular Kitchen

Modular Kitchens

Our modular kitchens are built for Indian cooking — with layouts that accommodate masala dabbas, pressure cookers, and tawas. We combine ergonomic design with premium hardware, durable countertops, and finishes that stand up to daily use. From L-shape to U-shape to island layouts, we make your kitchen a joy to work in.

  • Layout optimisation for Indian cooking
  • Premium hardware & soft-close fittings
  • Countertops & backsplash options
  • Smart storage & pull-out systems
  • Ventilation & lighting integration
Typical Duration 6–10 weeks
