Our Process

From Vision
to Reality

At Colored, every space begins with understanding and ends with perfection. Here's how we bring your home to life.

Scroll
01
Step 01

Discovery & Consultation

We begin by understanding your lifestyle, needs, and aspirations. This conversation forms the foundation of the design — your routines, your aesthetic instincts, and how you truly want to live in your space.

02
Step 02

Space Planning

Our team studies the layout and functionality of the space to create an efficient and beautiful spatial arrangement. Every square foot is considered — flow, light, proportion, and purpose.

03
Step 03

Design Concept

We develop mood boards, colour palettes, and design concepts that define the personality of the space. You see your home taking shape visually before a single material is ordered.

04
Step 04

Material & Finish Selection

Carefully selected materials, textures, and finishes bring depth, durability, and elegance to the design. We guide you through every choice — laminates, veneers, tiles, hardware, and paint.

05
Step 05

Execution & Coordination

Our team manages execution and works with skilled craftsmen to ensure the design is realised exactly as envisioned. Regular site visits, progress photos, and quality checks at every stage.

06
Step 06

Styling & Handover

Final styling elements are added — art, textiles, lighting accents — and the completed space is handed over ready to live in and enjoy. Warranty documentation and after-care support included.

C

Every project is a journey.
At Colored, we guide you through every step.
