Case Studies

Real Homes, Real Transformations

A glimpse into how we've helped homeowners across India turn their spaces into homes they love.

From Builder Flat to Dream Home

A young couple in Chennai with a 3 BHK builder flat wanted a modern, warm home that felt like them. COLORED redesigned the entire space in 14 weeks — including a modular kitchen and custom wardrobes — all within their budget. The result was a cohesive, livable interior that reflected their lifestyle without compromising on quality or timelines.

A Kitchen That Changed Everything

A family in Bangalore had an outdated kitchen that made cooking a chore. COLORED designed an ergonomic L-shaped modular kitchen with soft-close fittings and granite countertops. The project was completed in 7 weeks, and the family now enjoys a space that's both beautiful and highly functional for Indian cooking.

Villa Interiors Done Right

An NRI client needed their 4 BHK ECR villa fully designed and executed remotely. COLORED managed the entire process with virtual updates, site photos, and regular video calls. The team coordinated with local contractors and delivered a stunning modern-tropical interior — proving that distance is no barrier when you have the right partner.
