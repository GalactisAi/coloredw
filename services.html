Our Services

Detailed, personalized interior design services for every part of your home

Residential Interiors

Transform your apartment, villa, or independent home into a space that truly reflects your lifestyle. Whether you're moving into a new flat or refreshing your existing home, we handle everything from concept to handover.

Who it's for

Homeowners doing up new apartments, villas, or refreshing existing homes across Chennai, Bangalore, Hyderabad, and India's leading metros.

What's included

  • Space planning & layout design
  • Custom furniture design
  • Flooring, paint & false ceiling
  • Electrical layout & lighting design
  • Décor & styling

Our process

Consultation → Concept & moodboards → Detailed design & 3D renders → Execution & site management → Handover & after-care.

Timeline 10–16 weeks for full home

Kitchen Design

The heart of every Indian home deserves thoughtful design. We create kitchens that balance functionality with style — whether you're building from scratch or renovating an existing space.

Who it's for

Anyone building or renovating a kitchen — from compact apartment kitchens to spacious villa layouts.

What's included

  • Layout planning (L-shape, U-shape, island)
  • Cabinetry design & material selection
  • Countertops, backsplash & hardware
  • Appliance integration
  • Chimney & sink placement

Our process

Site visit & measurements → Layout options → Material & finish selection → 3D visualization → Fabrication & installation.

Timeline 6–10 weeks

Maximize every inch with smart, stylish storage. From walk-in closets to compact apartment wardrobes, we design solutions that keep your home organized and clutter-free.

Who it's for

Homeowners wanting organized, stylish storage — whether you have a spacious master bedroom or a compact 2 BHK.

What's included

  • Walk-in wardrobes
  • Sliding & hinged wardrobes
  • Loft storage
  • Dressing units & vanity
  • Shoe cabinets & accessory storage
Timeline 4–8 weeks
